About Me

I was 9 when I created my first video game. Actually it was a just a level on the classic game Lode Runner. But regardless, I was hooked. Then came:
Hello World


I still remember how cool it felt to make my computer spell out those words. Ever since, I've been focused on gaining the skills needed to be a game programmer. I have 5 years experience with Unity and have worked extensively with a variety of game design software.

I also write and perform comedy monologues. I've summited Mount St. Helens twice and have backpacked on month-long trips in the Northwest US and Canada on six separate occasions. Originally from Virginia, I now call Florida and North Carolina home.
